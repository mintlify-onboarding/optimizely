---
title: "Recipient export job"
description: "Exports a recipient list into a CSV file on a regular basis."
---

* The automated recipient export job exports a recipient list into a CSV file on a regular basis.
* Set the export frequency to daily, weekly or monthly.
* Export the complete list or only the changes since the last export.
* You can also export active recipients only.
